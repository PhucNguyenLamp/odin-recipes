Rhubarb Shrub

Make Rhubarb syrup (No. 4). Stir in 1 1/2 cups water, 1/2 cup lemon juice and 1/4 cup white vinegar. Cover; refrigerate 2 days. Stir; mix each 1/2 cup with 1/2 cup seltzer.

Ingredients

  • water
  • lemon juice
  • white vinegar
  • seltzer

Steps

  1. Stir in 1 1/2 cups water, 1/2 cup lemon juice and 1/4 cup white vinegar
  2. Cover; refrigerate 2 days
  3. Stir; mix each 1/2 cup with 1/2 cup seltzer.
