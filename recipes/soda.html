Cherry-Vanilla Soda

Simmer 2 cups cherry juice, 1/2 cup sugar and 1 split, scraped vanilla bean (seeds and pod) over medium heat until syrupy, 15 to 20 minutes; strain. Let cool completely. Mix with seltzer.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 4 tablespoons flavored syrup
  • 1 cup cold seltzer
  • ice

Steps

  1. mix flavored syrup with cold seltzer
  2. add ice
